Rey Ross

About

Rey Ross is DJ/Producer/Songwriter based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. His DJ sets span eras and genres while demanding that the crowd get up and move. He has performed around the Twin Cities including opening for acts such as Vic Mensa.

After years spent DJing and producing Rey Ross (Ross Nevin) is finally stepping out from behind the decks as a singer and songwriter. Accompanied by Nate LeBrun on drums and Toby Martin on the bass the group produces soulful rock music that evokes Michael Kiwanuka and Bill Withers. Ross seeks to write music exploring the most pressing questions facing humankind today while keeping butts bouncing and heads rocking.
